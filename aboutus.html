About Our Firm

Welcome to VALLIMEENA & ASSOCIATES, a trustworthy Chartered Accountant firm based in Tirupur, Tamilnadu, specialising in Taxation and Business advisory services.

About Our Firm

We provide the best accounting services

for individuals & businesses

  • VALLIMEENA & ASSOCIATES started on 19th October, 2018, on Vijayadasami with the mission of making it easier for entrepreneurs to develop their business.
  • Since then, we have been helping to start and operate thousands of businesses by offering a wide range of business services.
  • Our aim is to help entrepreneurs on the legal and regulatory requirements and be a partner throughout the business lifecycle, offering support at every stage to ensure the business remains compliant and continually growing.
  • We are dedicated to helping entrepreneurs and business owners easily start and grow their business by providing quality services at an affordable cost.
